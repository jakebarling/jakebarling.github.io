Guide

Guides and Tips on getting better at taking a photo.

Guides

Learn how to use your camera by reading the manual.

Use good composition by following basic guidelines
such as the rule of thirds,
leading lines, symmetry, and framing.

Look at the work of photographers you admire
and seek feedback to grow as a photographer.

Try new things and be creative to develop your unique style
that reflects your vision and personality.

Have fun using the camera because in the end of the day
you should have fun and not let anyone ruin your day.

Tips

Look for a different perspective by taking your photo
from an unusual angle or position to make it stand out.

Blur the background by using the aperture settings on your camera
to create a blurred background and make your subject stand out.

Choose a good background to make sure the background
doesn't distract from your subject and looks clean and simple.

Wait for the right moment and be patient and wait
for the perfect moment to take your photo.

Play with the exposure by adjusting the brightness and contrast of your photo
using the camera settings to make it look more interesting.
